ROSEMARY AMBER ~ From a young age Rosemary has been very passionate about two things: storytelling and documenting the passage of time. She is committed to use her work to investigate the complexities of contemporary social issues. Her work addresses the relationship between multitudes and individuals.
Rosemary thinks pandas are awesome.

ACKER MOONSTONE ~ Framing and composition are second nature to Acker but his strongest asset is the magical power to become invisible in the most daunting shooting situations. Surprisingly, his decade long experience in news production has not made him cynical.
Acker thinks pandas should rule the world.
